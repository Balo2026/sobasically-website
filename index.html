SO BASICALLY

A Winnipeg-based collective dedicated to amplifying local art and music culture.

We create spaces where artists, musicians, and creators connect, collaborate, and share their work with the community.

From intimate showcases to larger events, we're building a platform that celebrates the creative energy of Winnipeg and brings people together through art and sound.

Based in the heart of Winnipeg, we're committed to fostering a vibrant, inclusive scene where creativity thrives.
